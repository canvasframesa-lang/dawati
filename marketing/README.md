## How to use

Every doc is structured `## التاريخ → ## القناة → ## النسخة`.
Open the file, find the slot, copy the block, paste in the channel.

## The North Star

> «احنا نصمّم دعوتك، وأنت تستقبل ضيوفك.»

Every line of copy you write — internal or external — should land within
walking distance of this promise. If it doesn't, cut it.

## The two tones

| When | Tone |
|---|---|
| Marketing chrome (Snapchat, IG, ads, DMs) | Najdi conversational — warm, like a friend |
| The card itself + religious lines | Classical Arabic with full tashkeel |

Mixing tones inside one piece is fine if the religious moment is quoted.
Mixing on regular prose breaks the spell — pick one.

## What to avoid (always)

- Egyptian dialect markers (وش / ازاي / ده / كده) — wrong audience
- Levantine markers (هلق / كتير / منيح) — wrong audience
- Government-form Arabic (إن هذا التطبيق يتيح...) — boring
- Hard-sell language (اشتري الآن! فرصة محدودة!) — cheapens the brand
- Promo discounting as primary CTA — we're premium, not bargain
- Photos of brides without consent — cultural respect
